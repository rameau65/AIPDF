# Quality System

## Character QA

- Same face proportions across all views
- Stable silhouette and hairstyle
- Connected front, side, and back costume construction
- Distinct, non-duplicated expressions and poses
- Readable identity at thumbnail size

## World and Story QA

- Every location has an emotional purpose
- Locations connect to episodes, products, and experiences
- Relationships generate behavior and conflict
- World rules remain internally consistent

## Brand QA

- Logo, color, typography, and layout rules are documented
- Information hierarchy is clear
- Graphic motifs can be reused across channels
- Character-free pages still feel like the same brand

## Product QA

- Manufacturable part structure
- Stable center of gravity
- Packaging, shipping, and retail constraints considered
- Entry, core, premium, and experience tiers are differentiated

## Publishing QA

- PPTX remains editable
- PDF is print-safe
- DOCX matches the latest approved content
- Names, metrics, dates, and roadmaps are synchronized
- Assumptions and confirmed facts are visibly separated
- Rights, credits, and third-party marks are reviewed
