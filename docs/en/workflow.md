# End-to-End Workflow

## Stage 1 — Character Identity
Define Character DNA, silhouette, facial proportions, turnaround, expressions, poses, costume, scale, and consistency constraints.

## Stage 2 — Visual Asset Library
Build Emotion Bloom™, relationship maps, world maps, story loops, brand diagrams, color systems, iconography, patterns, and reusable presentation assets.

## Stage 3 — Product Visualization
Create art-toy renders, packaging, blind boxes, collector editions, merchandise, retail displays, pop-up spaces, planograms, and launch calendars.

## Stage 4 — Final Publishing
Publish an investor PPTX, print/distribution PDF, editable DOCX manuscript, asset index, rights notice, version record, and delivery checklist.

## Stage 5 — Commercialization
Prepare licensing categories, partner packages, pricing tiers, channel strategy, content calendars, launch scenarios, and performance metrics.

## Stage Gates

Each stage must pass four gates before moving forward:

1. Identity consistency
2. Production feasibility
3. Brand coherence
4. Commercial relevance
